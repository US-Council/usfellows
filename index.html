USIVA Fellows Program

USIVA partners with immigrant fellows — anyone with a completed high-school degree — to build a clear path from Bachelor's, Master's, or PhD study into an F-1 OPT or J-1 fellowship, and from there toward long-term doorways like EB-2 NIW and H-1B. We connect fellows to unpaid fellowship placements with partner organizations, show them exactly what paperwork postures them for each step, and walk the whole journey with them as lifelong fellows.
