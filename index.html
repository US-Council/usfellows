Our Mission

Talent in service to missions larger than self

US Fellows is a national civic fellowship society that honors and connects exceptional people across disciplines to humanity-scale missions through public-interest projects, research affiliations, NGO partnerships, and service-oriented fellowships.

01

Honor

Recognizing individuals whose talent, character, and commitment reflect the highest ideals of public service.

Mission

Connecting every fellowship appointment to meaningful work of enduring public consequence.

03

Stewardship

Strengthening institutions, supporting public-benefit work, and serving future generations.

Why US Fellows Exists

The great challenges of our time require exceptional people willing to serve.

Across the country and around the world, mission-driven institutions are working on scientific discovery, human dignity, civic trust, climate resilience, education, immigration, technology, public health, humanitarian service, and national capacity.

US Fellows creates a distinguished civic pathway for people who want to contribute their abilities to these missions with seriousness, honor, and institutional support.
