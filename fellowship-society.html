From intent to practice

How the Society stays active

The Society is sustained through recurring practices that turn affiliation into useful relationships and continued service.

Fellows and alumni establishes the point of entry; mission partners defines what should remain after the immediate work is complete.

Welcome

Orient Fellows to the standard, one another, and the missions that organize the Society's work. Here, attend to fellows and alumni: Carry the standard through appointments, professional life, mentorship, and later Society missions.

Connect

Build trusted peer, advisor, host, and alumni relationships across disciplines and places. Here, attend to advisors: Offer domain knowledge, judgment, introductions, and guidance without displacing accountable leadership.

Convene

Create serious forums for learning, collaboration, reflection, and recognition of contribution. Here, attend to host institutions: Anchor fellowship in real work and connect the Society to active public-interest needs.

Continue

Carry fellowship forward through mentorship, public-interest work, and future Society missions. Here, attend to mission partners: Extend reach, context, and collaboration across communities, disciplines, and institutions.
