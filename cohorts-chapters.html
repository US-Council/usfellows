From intent to practice

How the Society stays active

The Society is sustained through recurring practices that turn affiliation into useful relationships and continued service.

Welcome

Orient Fellows to the standard, one another, and the missions that organize the Society's work. Here, attend to cohort formation: Create a trusted peer group for orientation, reflection, exchange, and mutual accountability.

Connect

Build trusted peer, advisor, host, and alumni relationships across disciplines and places. Here, attend to regional connection: Bring Fellows and institutions together around local knowledge, needs, and opportunities for service.

Convene

Create serious forums for learning, collaboration, reflection, and recognition of contribution. Here, attend to mission circles: Convene across geography when a field or public challenge benefits from sustained specialist exchange.

Continue

Carry fellowship forward through mentorship, public-interest work, and future Society missions. Here, attend to alumni continuity: Maintain relationships through mentorship, referrals, chapter leadership, and renewed contribution.

These tests connect public purpose to responsible practice.

03

A lasting standard

The Code of Service continues to guide conduct beyond a single cohort or appointment. In this context, consider mission circles.
