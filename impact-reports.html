From intent to practice

From experience to a useful contribution

Journal work is shaped to preserve rigor while remaining accessible to people working across different disciplines and institutions.

Frame

Identify the question, audience, relevance, and form best suited to the material. Here, attend to contribution delivered: Document outputs, responsibilities completed, quality, reach, and how the work was used.

Develop

Gather evidence, context, field insight, and competing perspectives with appropriate care. Here, attend to institutional value: Examine capability, relationships, knowledge, or systems strengthened through the appointment.

Review

Clarify claims, limitations, permissions, confidentiality, and the practical value of publication. Here, attend to mission progress: Connect near-term results to the larger public-interest purpose without claiming sole attribution.

Publish and extend

Share the work and use it to support discussion, learning, and future inquiry across the Society. Here, attend to learning and accountability: Name challenges, unintended effects, feedback, corrections, and priorities for future work.

A disciplined account of contribution and learning

Not every result can be reduced to one metric. Credible reporting combines proportionate evidence with context, limitations, stakeholder perspective, and attention to longer-term capacity.

Contribution delivered establishes the point of entry; learning and accountability defines what should remain after the immediate work is complete.

The standard

Principles that hold the work

These tests connect public purpose to responsible practice.

01

Earn the reader's trust

Distinguish observation, evidence, interpretation, and recommendation with care. In this context, consider contribution delivered.

02

Write from substance

Ground contributions in credible inquiry, direct practice, or a clearly articulated argument. In this context, consider institutional value.

03

Serve a larger conversation

Help Fellows, institutions, and the public understand a mission or act on it more responsibly. In this context, consider mission progress.

Put this work into practice

Begin with contribution delivered, then define a contribution your capabilities or institution can support.
