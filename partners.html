Who Delivers The Program

Partner Organizations

US Fellows is run by USIVA together with three partner NGOs. Each contributes fellowship placements and mentorship in its own field, giving fellows a real choice of where to start.

Tao Learning Education

Tao Learning contributes education-focused fellowship placements to the program — a fit for fellows whose background or degree points toward teaching, curriculum, or learning-technology work.

Curiosity Research Corporation Research

Curiosity Research Corporation contributes research-oriented fellowship placements — a fit for fellows coming out of graduate or doctoral programs looking to build a US research record.

INSTAR Lab Inc Applied Science & Labs

INSTAR Lab Inc contributes lab and applied-science fellowship placements — a fit for fellows with a technical or applied-science background looking for hands-on work.

How It Works

The unpaid-placement, foot-in-the-door model

Matched to a Partner

After a consult, USIVA matches fellows to the partner organization whose field best fits their degree and goals — Tao Learning, Curiosity Research Corporation, or INSTAR Lab Inc.

Placed, Not Just Introduced

The fellowship is a structured, unpaid placement with the partner organization — built specifically to give candidates without US work history their first real credential.

Guided Through the Visa Track

USIVA stays involved through the F-1 OPT or J-1 process that accompanies the placement, alongside the partner organization hosting the fellowship.

A Relationship That Continues

Placements are a starting point, not an end point — many fellows stay connected to their partner organization well past the initial fellowship.
