Who Delivers The Program

Partner Organizations

US Fellows is run by USIVA together with three partner NGOs — Tao Learning, Curiosity Research Corporation, and INSTAR Lab Inc. USIVA handles the visa track; each partner hosts the unpaid fellowship placement itself, in its own field. Below is what each organization actually does, so fellows can see where they'd be working before they apply.

The Three Partners

Where a placement is actually spent

Education

Tao Learning

Tao Learning is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit built around a P-20+ initiative — advancing education across the full continuum from early learning through doctoral study and into the workforce, with a STEAM focus and a standing commitment to literacy across written, spoken, digital, and emerging forms of language.

Focus Areas

  • P-20+ curriculum and pedagogy development
  • Literacy advocacy across written, spoken, and digital language
  • STEAM-focused instructional resources tied to workforce forecasting
  • The Tao Learning Consortium, its network of partner organizations

What Fellows Do Here

Placements sit inside Tao Learning's active education work — curriculum and pedagogy projects, literacy programs, and STEAM instructional resources. It's a fit for fellows whose degree points toward teaching, curriculum design, or learning-technology work.

taolearning.org
Research

Curiosity Research Corporation

Curiosity Research Corporation studies the psychology of curiosity — in human learners and in machine systems — and turns that research into pedagogy: the methods that keep learners motivated as classroom models evolve. Its mission, stated plainly on its own site, is to make the world more curious.

Focus Areas

  • Ignite Curiosity, its student-facing discovery program
  • Curiosity Camps and Curiosity Cottages, bootcamp and physical-space programs
  • Curiosity Hive, its learner community, and Curiosity Coaches, its mentors
  • DreamTrack, its portfolio system for auditable, career-ready progress

What Fellows Do Here

Placements are a fit for fellows coming out of graduate or doctoral programs who want to build a US research record — working on curiosity and pedagogy research, coaching, or program design across its Camps, Hive, and Coaches programs.

curiositycorp.org
Applied Science & Labs

INSTAR Lab Inc

INSTAR Lab is an independent 501(c)(3) nonprofit research institute advancing science through interdisciplinary collaboration, technological innovation, and partnership — accountable, as a public charity, to the public interest rather than to shareholders or a single funder.

Focus Areas

  • AI & machine intelligence, quantum science, and data analytics
  • Health, energy, and high-performance computing research
  • Earth & space, life, social, and physical sciences
  • Its own "Chart. Explore. Transmit." research method, PI-led end to end

What Fellows Do Here

Placements are a fit for fellows with a technical or applied-science background looking for hands-on, PI-led research work. INSTAR runs its own citizen-scientist fellowship model in parallel, training researchers at every level with no minimum degree requirement.

instarlab.org
How It Works

The unpaid-placement, foot-in-the-door model

Matched to a Partner

After a consult, USIVA matches fellows to the partner organization whose field best fits their degree and goals — Tao Learning, Curiosity Research Corporation, or INSTAR Lab Inc.

Placed, Not Just Introduced

The fellowship is a structured, unpaid placement with the partner organization — built specifically to give candidates without US work history their first real credential.

Guided Through the Visa Track

USIVA stays involved through the F-1 OPT or J-1 process that accompanies the placement, alongside the partner organization hosting the fellowship.

A Relationship That Continues

Placements are a starting point, not an end point. Fellows keep standing in their partner organization's network long after the fellowship ends — a lifelong connection, not a one-time credential.
