Research in service to mission

One scholarly standard. Five levels of responsibility.

The US Fellows R&D Scholars Program connects developing researchers, experienced technical professionals, and nationally significant scientific leaders to responsible, public-interest research.

Designation pathway

Placement reflects present eligibility—not an automatic ladder

Each designation describes a distinct level of access, commitment, responsibility, and demonstrated leadership. Applicants should choose the level they can substantiate now.

01
Flexible · part-time · international

International R&D Scholar

A voluntary, self-paced pathway for students, independent researchers, engineers, technologists, and citizen scientists building credible research and proposal networks.

Explore the full program
  • No fixed weekly commitment
  • Thesis and literature development
  • Science communication and technical writing
  • Data validation, workshops, and proposal support
02
Structured · mentored · 20+ hours weekly

Resident R&D Scholar

A professional pathway preparing researchers for rigorous applied R&D in national laboratories, FFRDCs, agencies, and other mission institutions.

Explore the full program
  • Research methods and documentation
  • Cybersecurity and responsible AI literacy
  • Scientific communication and proposals
  • Ethics, collaboration, and teamwork
03
Active clearance · full-time · national mission

Trusted R&D Scholar

A full-time designation for eligible professionals contributing to projects of U.S. national importance under applicable security, credential, and compliance requirements.

Explore the full program
  • Active U.S. Government clearance required
  • Continuing eligibility and credential review
  • Security and confidentiality discipline
  • Responsible work in sensitive environments
04
Funded leadership · multidisciplinary programs

Principal R&D Scholar

For current or former PIs, Co-PIs, Key Personnel, program or technical directors, chief scientists, and comparable leaders of nationally important work.

Explore the full program
  • Funded research and proposal leadership
  • Team and partnership stewardship
  • Technical and program direction
  • Translation of research to mission use
05
Highest recognition · decades of impact

Distinguished R&D Scholar

Recognition for sustained national-impact work across academia, industry, government, or defense, paired with meaningful continued contribution to US Fellows.

Explore the full program
  • Major publications and leadership
  • Cross-sector influence and mentorship
  • Continuing strategic, intellectual, institutional, service, or financial contribution
  • Financial support alone cannot purchase the title
Shared standards

Capability joined to integrity and service

Selection and continued standing consider the whole record, the proposed contribution, and the responsibilities attached to the designation.

Research integrity

Accurate methods, responsible evidence use, attribution, reproducibility, and honest representation of results.

Responsible technology

Appropriate use of AI, data, cybersecurity practices, confidentiality, and export-control safeguards.

Service and collaboration

Mission alignment, constructive teamwork, communication, mentorship, and contribution beyond personal advancement.

Leadership and evidence

Publications, innovation, program responsibility, partnerships, and demonstrated translation from knowledge to use.

Entry and advancement

Recognition is reviewed, not assumed

People may enter through application, invitation, nomination, or institutional partnership. Advancement is never automatic; each designation requires evidence of its own eligibility gate and contribution standard.

01

Review

Program fit, integrity, capability, service, and supporting evidence are evaluated.

02

Designation

Terms, scope, title use, permissions, and any institution-specific conditions are made explicit.

03

Periodic standing

Active participation may be reviewed and moved to inactive, alumni, or emeritus standing when appropriate.

Important boundaries

A Scholar designation is not a promise of access or placement

Participation does not grant employment, compensation, visa sponsorship, admission, funding, security clearance, restricted-system or laboratory access, proposal participation, or advancement. Publication, intellectual-property, confidentiality, security, and export-control requirements remain in force.

Never submit classified, controlled, proprietary, or otherwise sensitive details through the public application.
