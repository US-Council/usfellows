[PREAMBLE]
Institutional editorial photography for a national civic fellowship society (US Fellows). Every image is an atmospheric, medium-format environmental shot of an interior or exterior setting relevant to public-interest service, research, governance, and fellowship. Deep, restrained navy and blue shadows with selective warm gold highlights. Natural window light or soft diffused studio lighting — never harsh or flat. Subjects are settings (archives, reading rooms, institutional hallways, research libraries, civic buildings, field stations, laboratories, lecture halls, seminar rooms, courtyards, and landscapes) rendered with compositional precision and a quiet, dignified mood. Evoke the gravitas of public service, scholarship, and institutional stewardship. Color palette: deep navy (#071d3b), warm gold (#c5a45a), restrained cream, and off-white tones. Architecture and environments only — no human faces or recognizable figures. Framing is deliberate and structured, using symmetry, leading lines, and geometric composition. Shallow depth of field where it serves focus. Always a sense of purpose and permanence. No digital or sci-fi elements. Every scene must feel like a real place where consequential work happens.
