# Image Manifest

The current site intentionally uses one licensed architectural image from the Kingster template package.

| Asset | Dimensions | Use |
| --- | --- | --- |
| `public/assets/img_hero_campus.jpg` | 1800 × 981 | Homepage hero, inner-page title banners, and institutional split feature |

The image receives a dark navy overlay in CSS so headings meet contrast requirements and the treatment remains consistent with the US Fellows visual identity.

Do not add portraits presented as actual Fellows, leaders, advisors, or Host Institution representatives unless US Council supplies and approves the source image and identity. New editorial photography should communicate service, research, civic institutions, education, science, and public-interest work without implying undocumented partnerships or appointments.
